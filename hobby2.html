The Smothered Mate

The smothered mate is a checkmate tactic that revolves around trapping the opponent's king with its own pieces, hence the name smothered. I find this move particularly fascinating because it's a rare occurrence where you achieve checkmate using only a knight. This strategic maneuver often entails sacrificing one of your other pieces, forcing your opponent to capture it with either their rook or knight, unwittingly falling into the trap.
