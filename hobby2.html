SMOTHERED MATE

The smothered mate is a checkmate tactic that revolves around using your horse to trap the opponent's king with his own pieces, hence the name smothered.

TWO KNIGHTS MATE

This is another checkmate with two knights, and a king. It is apparently the rarest checkmate.
