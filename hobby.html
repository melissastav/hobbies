SWIMMING

Swimming is a great full-body exercise. I swim every day, and I've noticed gradual improvement. I particularly enjoy swimming in the sea, especially on stormy days. One of my favorite challenges is testing how far I can swim underwater. To achieve this, I take a few rapid breaths for about a minute before submerging. This pre-breathing saturates my blood with oxygen, allowing me to stay submerged for longer without needing to inhale. Typically, we gasp for air unnecessarily; it's the body's natural reaction to a perceived lack of oxygen in the blood. Therefore, by pre-breathing quickly, the body can endure longer periods underwater.
